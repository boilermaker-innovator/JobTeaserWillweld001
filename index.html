Full Job Description

Position: Fitter/Mechanic

Location: Kalgoorlie and throughout the Goldfields, WA

Company: Willweld - Specialists in heavy earth moving machinery repairs across the Western Australian mining industry

About the Role:

As a Fitter/Mechanic at Willweld, you will be responsible for the repair, maintenance, and servicing of heavy earth moving equipment. You'll work both on-site and in our Kalgoorlie-Boulder workshop, ensuring the reliability and efficiency of our clients' machinery.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Diagnose, repair, and maintain heavy earth moving equipment
  • Perform preventative maintenance and servicing
  • Troubleshoot mechanical, hydraulic, and electrical systems
  • Work collaboratively with other trades, including boilermakers and electricians
  • Ensure all work meets safety standards and quality requirements

Requirements:

  • Certificate III in Engineering - Mechanical Trade or equivalent
  • Proven experience working with heavy earth-moving equipment
  • Strong problem-solving and diagnostic skills
  • Ability to read and interpret technical manuals and schematics
  • Current driver's license (HR license preferred)
  • Willingness to work in remote locations when required
  • Strong commitment to safety and quality workmanship

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary package
  • Opportunity for both FIFO and residential positions
  • Career development and training opportunities
  • Work with a diverse range of mining equipment
  • Be part of a growing company in the WA mining sector
  • Potential relocation assistance for the right candidates

Willweld is an equal opportunity employer committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace.
